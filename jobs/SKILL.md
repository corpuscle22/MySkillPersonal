## Job Search Workflow

### Step 1: Search Job Sites

Search the following top US job sites for physician informatician positions. Prioritize sites in tier order:

**Tier 1 - Healthcare-Specific:**
1. **Health eCareers** (healthecareers.com) - Primary healthcare job board
2. **AMIA Career Center** (careers.amia.org) - AMIA's official job board
3. **HIMSS JobMine** (jobmine.himss.org) - Health IT focused

**Tier 2 - General Job Boards:**
4. **LinkedIn Jobs** (linkedin.com/jobs) - Largest professional network
5. **Indeed** (indeed.com) - Comprehensive job aggregator
6. **Glassdoor** (glassdoor.com) - Jobs + company reviews

7. **Doximity** (doximity.com/careers) - Physician-specific network
