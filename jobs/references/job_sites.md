# Healthcare Job Sites Reference

Comprehensive guide to US job sites for Physician Informatician positions, organized by category and priority.

## Tier 1: Healthcare-Specific Job Boards

### AMIA Career Center
- **URL:** https://careers.amia.org
- **Why Important:** AMIA (American Medical Informatics Association) is THE professional organization for clinical informaticians. This is the most targeted job board.
- **Search Tips:**
  - Direct searches for "Physician Informatician", "CMIO"
  - Filter by job type, location, experience level
  - Many academic medical center postings
- **Best For:** Academic positions, health system CMIO roles, research positions

### HIMSS Job Board (JobMine)
- **URL:** https://jobmine.himss.org
- **Why Important:** HIMSS focuses on health IT leadership and technology
- **Search Tips:**
  - Search "Clinical Informatics", "Medical Director"
  - Filter by organization type (vendor, provider, payer)
- **Best For:** Health IT vendor positions, CIO/CMIO roles, consulting

### Health eCareers
- **URL:** https://www.healthecareers.com
- **Why Important:** Large healthcare-focused job aggregator
- **Search Tips:**
  - Use specialty filter: "Informatics"
  - Search: "Physician Informatician", "Medical Informatics"
- **Best For:** Broad healthcare roles, both clinical and informatics

### Doximity
- **URL:** https://www.doximity.com
- **Why Important:** Physician-specific network with job listings
- **Search Tips:**
  - Must have physician account
  - Job recommendations based on profile
  - Strong for locum and permanent positions
- **Best For:** Physician-specific roles, networking with hiring managers

### PracticeLink
- **URL:** https://www.practicelink.com
- **Why Important:** Physician recruitment platform
- **Search Tips:**
  - Create physician profile
  - Filter by specialty and role type
- **Best For:** Traditional physician roles, some informatics

## Tier 2: General Professional Job Boards

### LinkedIn Jobs
- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/jobs
- **Why Important:** Largest professional network, many healthcare IT companies post here
- **Search Tips:**
  - Use quotes: "Physician Informatician", "Clinical Informaticist"
  - Set alerts for key terms
  - Filter: Remote, Easy Apply, Date Posted
  - Check company pages directly
- **Best For:** Tech companies, startups, network-based discovery

### Indeed
- **URL:** https://www.indeed.com
- **Why Important:** Largest job aggregator, comprehensive coverage
- **Search Tips:**
  - Advanced search: title contains "Physician" AND "Informatics"
  - Filter by salary range ($250K+)
  - Check "Remote" filter
  - Sort by date to see newest
- **Best For:** Comprehensive search, salary data

### Glassdoor
- **URL:** https://www.glassdoor.com/Job
- **Why Important:** Jobs + company reviews + salary data
- **Search Tips:**
  - Search jobs while researching companies
  - Check company ratings before applying
  - Review interview experiences
- **Best For:** Company research, salary benchmarking

### ZipRecruiter
- **URL:** https://www.ziprecruiter.com
- **Why Important:** AI-powered matching, good for getting noticed
- **Search Tips:**
  - Upload resume for matching
  - "One-Click Apply" feature
- **Best For:** Passive job seeking, getting discovered

## Tier 3: Executive & Health Tech Focused

### Built In
- **URL:** https://builtin.com
- **Why Important:** Tech company focused, strong health tech presence
- **Search Tips:**
  - Filter by Health Tech industry
  - Check city-specific sites (Built In Chicago, etc.)
  - Look for Series B+ companies
- **Best For:** Health tech startups, product-focused roles

### Wellfound (formerly AngelList)
- **URL:** https://wellfound.com
- **Why Important:** Startup ecosystem, equity compensation
- **Search Tips:**
  - Filter: Healthcare, Series A-D
  - Check for physician/clinician roles
- **Best For:** Early-stage startups, equity opportunities

### Heidrick & Struggles / Korn Ferry / Russell Reynolds
- **Why Important:** Executive search firms for C-suite roles
- **How to Engage:**
  - Submit CV through websites
  - Network with healthcare practice partners
  - Often handle CMIO, CMO searches
- **Best For:** CMIO, CMO, VP-level positions

### Rock Health Jobs
- **URL:** https://rockhealth.com/jobs (or similar health tech VC job boards)
- **Why Important:** Digital health VC ecosystem
- **Search Tips:**
  - Check portfolio company jobs
  - Subscribe to newsletters
- **Best For:** Digital health startups

## Search Strategy

### Boolean Search Strings

For LinkedIn/Indeed:
```
"Physician Informatician" OR "Clinical Informaticist" OR "CMIO" OR "Chief Medical Informatics Officer"
```

```
("Medical Director" OR "Clinical Director") AND ("Informatics" OR "Digital Health" OR "AI")
```

```
"MD" AND ("Clinical Informatics" OR "Health Informatics") AND ("Remote" OR "Hybrid")
```

### Recommended Search Cadence

| Site | Frequency | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| AMIA Career Center | Weekly | Set email alerts |
| LinkedIn | Daily | Save searches, enable alerts |
| Indeed | 2x weekly | Sort by date |
| Target companies | Monthly | Check directly |

### Job Alert Setup

Set up automated alerts on:
1. LinkedIn - "Physician Informatician", "CMIO", "Clinical Informatics Director"
2. Indeed - Same terms with $200K+ salary filter
3. AMIA - All clinical informatics roles
4. Glassdoor - Target companies
