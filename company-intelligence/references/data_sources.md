# Data Sources Reference

Comprehensive list of data sources for company intelligence research.

---

## Free APIs (Configured in this Skill)

### SEC EDGAR
- **URL**: https://www.sec.gov/edgar
- **API**: https://data.sec.gov
- **Cost**: Free, no key required
- **Coverage**: All US public companies
- **Best for**: Official financial filings, annual/quarterly reports

**Available data:**
- 10-K (annual reports)
- 10-Q (quarterly reports)
- 8-K (material events)
- DEF 14A (proxy statements)
- S-1 (IPO registrations)
- Insider transactions

### Financial Modeling Prep (FMP)
- **URL**: https://financialmodelingprep.com
- **Cost**: Free tier (250 calls/day)
- **Coverage**: Global public companies
- **Best for**: Company profiles, financial metrics, ratios

**Available data:**
- Company profiles and descriptions
- Income statements
- Balance sheets
- Cash flow statements
- Key financial metrics
- Stock quotes
- Company search

### Alpha Vantage
- **URL**: https://www.alphavantage.co
- **Cost**: Free tier (25 calls/day)
- **Coverage**: Global stocks
- **Best for**: Stock prices, technical data

**Available data:**
- Real-time quotes
- Historical prices
- Technical indicators
- Fundamental data
- Forex/crypto data

### OpenCorporates
- **URL**: https://opencorporates.com
- **Cost**: Free tier (500 calls/month)
- **Coverage**: 140+ jurisdictions worldwide
- **Best for**: Company registry data

**Available data:**
- Incorporation details
- Officers and directors
- Company numbers
- Filing history
- Corporate relationships

## Web Sources (Manual Research)

### Crunchbase
- **URL**: https://crunchbase.com
- **Coverage**: Startups and tech companies primarily
- **Best for**: Funding rounds, investors, valuations

**Available data (public profiles):**
- Company overview
- Funding history
- Investors
- Key people
- Acquisitions
- News mentions

**Access tips:**
- Public profiles available without account
- Create free account for more data
- Pro subscription needed for full access

### PitchBook
- **URL**: https://pitchbook.com
- **Coverage**: Private equity, VC, M&A
- **Best for**: Detailed private company financials

**Available data:**
- Funding details
- Valuations
- Cap tables
- Exit data
- Comparable transactions

**Access tips:**
- Limited free access
- University library access often available
- Subscription required for full data

### LinkedIn
- **URL**: https://linkedin.com/company/
- **Coverage**: All companies with presence
- **Best for**: Employee counts, growth trends

**Available data:**
- Employee count
- Employee growth trends
- Key employees
- Company updates
- Job postings (hiring trends)

### CB Insights
- **URL**: https://cbinsights.com
- **Coverage**: Private companies, emerging tech
- **Best for**: Market maps, industry analysis

### Yahoo Finance
- **URL**: https://finance.yahoo.com
- **Coverage**: Global public companies
- **Best for**: Stock data, analyst estimates

**Available data:**
- Stock quotes and charts
- Financial statements
- Analyst recommendations
- Institutional holders
- Insider transactions

### Google Finance
- **URL**: https://google.com/finance
- **Coverage**: Global public companies
- **Best for**: Quick stock lookup, news

## Data Quality Considerations

### Primary Sources (Highest Trust)
1. SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q)
2. Company press releases
3. Regulatory filings
4. Court documents

### Secondary Sources (Moderate Trust)
1. Reputable news outlets
2. Industry analysts
3. Academic research

### Tertiary Sources (Verify Claims)
1. Company websites
2. Social media
3. Blog posts
4. Wikipedia

### Always Cross-Reference
- Verify funding data across multiple sources
- Check SEC filings for official financial data
- Confirm news against primary sources
- Note date of data (may be outdated)
